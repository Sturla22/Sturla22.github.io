Indoor / Simulator

When enabled: after hitting the green, enter your proximity and putts are auto-assigned (≤ 3 m → 1 putt; 3–20 m → 2 putts; > 20 m → 3 putts). Fixed putts count toward your score but are excluded from putting SG stats.
